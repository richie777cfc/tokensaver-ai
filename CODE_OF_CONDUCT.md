# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and specific. Good collaboration here means:

- assume good intent
- focus on the code and the idea, not the person
- give reproducible reports and actionable suggestions
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that is abusive, hostile, or clearly off-topic.
